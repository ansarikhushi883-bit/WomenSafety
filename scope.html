Scope of Work
A clearly defined scope ensures focused, high-quality development and on-time delivery in Phase 1 of the SafeHer platform.

✅ In-Scope (Phase 1)

Web-based platform — desktop & mobile responsive
Emergency SOS request and alert system
Volunteer, support team, and authority coordination
Live location sharing and incident tracking
User profile and emergency contact management
Admin dashboard and reporting tools

❌ Out of Scope (Phase 1)

Native iOS or Android mobile application
Direct integration with police emergency systems
Wearable or IoT device integration
AI-based predictive risk assessment
Voice-activated SOS alerts

🗓️ Phase Roadmap

1

Phase 1 (Current): Web platform — SOS alerts, live location, volunteer coordination, admin dashboard

2

Phase 2: Native mobile app with SOS widget, push notifications, offline support

3

Phase 3: Law enforcement integration, AI risk prediction, wearable device support
